◎ Implication
Mid and High scenarios exceed single-event capacity
At mid (540), a single Q2 event is at the edge of venue capacity with no buffer for late registrations. At high (674), it's structurally impossible without a split event. The Double-Header model (two Day 1 events per quarter) is the recommended design. Q2 planning should assume mid-high and build in a second Day 1 cohort from the start.
◎ Composition
64% of Q2 deferred are ICs (35 of 55)
Consistent with the core NEC audience. Also 9 Managers and 5 Senior Managers are deferred — meaning Q2 Day 2 already has ~14 leader-track attendees before any new Q2 leaders are added. Factor this into Day 2 capacity planning separately.
◎ Function Mix

Content (6), Marketing (5), and Games (4) round out the top 4. No function should be surprised by their Q2 deferred headcount — HRBPs can set expectations with managers proactively now, before Q2 invites go out.
